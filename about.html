About Me

Hi, I’m Tilly (or Tala). I’m a Palestinian girl born in 2009, currently living in Amman Jordan. I’ve never stepped foot in Palestine, yet my heart lives in it.

I’ve been drawn to coding and the internet world for as long as I can remember. I’m a book lover, a poem writer, and someone who believes words can hold more than we expect.

“We don’t write our poems and thoughts they write and shape us.”

I created this project to have a safe space for people like me those who write from feelings from experinces,from pains, from honesty and from a simple love for words. Here, your poems don’t need names. They just need to exist.
